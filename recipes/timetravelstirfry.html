Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Eternal Returns
  • One Onion Only
  • Time
  • Optional: past, present, future
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Stir-fry the Vegetables with the Onion
  2. Go back in Time and stir-fry again
  3. Repeat as many times as necessary, until perfection
  4. Add Tomatoes
  5. Do not go too far into the past in order to fix your mistakes, it's a bad idea
  6. Enjoy!
